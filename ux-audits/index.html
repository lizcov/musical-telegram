City of Refuge Website Audit

My Role

UX/UI Analyst

My Contribution

  • UX Audit
  • Accessibility Analysis (WCAG 2.1)
  • User Flow Analysis
  • Strategic Recommendations

Date

2025

I performed a brief audit of the City of Refuge website, focusing on key aspects of user experience, from accessibility to the primary user flows. The audit identified both significant strengths and one critical area for improvement.

✅ Strengths: Delighting the Donor

The site's primary call-to-action, the donation button, is prominent and easy to find. The subsequent pop-up donation form is particularly well-designed: it's delightful, simple to use, and includes dynamic content that makes the user's experience personal and engaging. This highlights a clear understanding of the user's primary intent and a commitment to removing friction from the most important conversion path. The volunteer sign-up process was also seamless and engaging.

❌ Opportunity: Color Contrast and Accessibility

The most critical issue found was the low color contrast ratio of the text, particularly on buttons and over branded images. My analysis confirmed a contrast ratio of **2.51:1**, which falls significantly below the required **4.5:1** for normal text under **WCAG 2.1 AA** guidelines. This accessibility issue makes the site difficult to read for users with low vision, color blindness, or those in brightly lit environments.

The Solution: A Strategic Design Opportunity

The identified contrast issue is a major but straightforward fix. The solution is to ensure all text meets or exceeds the WCAG 2.1 AA standard for contrast. This could be achieved by using a darker brand color for text, adding an opaque overlay behind text on images, or adjusting the placement of text.

The Impact of Accessibility

By addressing this accessibility issue, the City of Refuge can immediately improve the user experience for a broad range of visitors. A more readable and accessible site reduces user frustration, strengthens the organization's brand reputation, and ultimately has the potential to increase engagement and donations by ensuring that all users can successfully complete their desired actions on the site.
